Things I learned this week:

1. Octopuses have three hearts and blue blood.
2. The shortest war in history lasted 38–45 minutes (Anglo-Zanzibar War, 1896).
3. Cleopatra lived closer in time to the Moon landing than to the construction of the Great Pyramid.
4. A group of flamingos is called a flamboyance.
5. Honey never spoils — edible honey has been found in 3000-year-old Egyptian tombs.

None of this is useful. All of it is wonderful.
